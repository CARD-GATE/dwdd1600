Find The Gear That Is Meant For You, Without The Confusion

What's So Confusing?

With the progression of the ever growing world of snow sports, we now have more brands, technologies, and ski and snowboard shapes and sizes than ever before. With this constant progression and increasing pollution of the industry, we are presented with an increasingly difficult decision when considering new gear. Any skiier or snowboarder interested in new gear can either decide to do copious amounts of research and spend heaps of money and time to discover the perfect gear for them, or find a local store with professionals to lead them in the right direction; otherwise we are left up to the devices of marketing and coporate money making schemes.

How Can We Help?

Here at TRUE CARVE we aim to reduce the noise and confusion of the industry by guiding skiiers and snowboarders through a series of relevant questions, giving us as well as the rider important insights into what shape, size, and tech specs would work best for their personal riding style. That way we can filter an incredibly large industry of equipment down to just to the gear that we're confident you'll love.

What Kind Of Info Do We Use?

  1. Height & Weight
  2. Boot Size
  3. Ride Style
  4. Terrain Preferences
  5. Level of Experience
  6. Other Important Info

We will still aim to give you a proper selection, because we know that snowsports are a powerful form of self expression, and in the end it all comes down to the style and graphic, and that decision belongs to you!
