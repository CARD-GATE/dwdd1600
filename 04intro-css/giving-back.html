Here Are Some Other Ways To Give Back

Donating used gear
If you're replacing your old gear and it is still useable, consider donating it to a nonprofit organization or passing it on to friends or family looking to start skiing or snowboarding.
Donate to snowsport oriented nonprofits
Consider donating to an organization such as POW, Share Winter Foundation, or The Chill Foundation.
Help your peers discover snowsports
Spread the word! With your new gear, reach out to friends, family, and whoever else and help them get on the slopes along with you, and with them we can make snowsports more accessable and inclusive.
